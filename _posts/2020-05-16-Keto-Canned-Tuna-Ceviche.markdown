### Ingredients
* 3-4 cans of tuna
* 1 tomato
* some cilantro
* 1 onion, or red onion
* lime juice
* Tabasco, to taste
* Avocado

### Instructions
1. Combine all Ingredients
2. Salt and pepper to taste
3. Top with avocado
